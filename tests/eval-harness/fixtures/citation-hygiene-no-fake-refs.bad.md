Log-structured key-value stores trace back to the LSM-tree design of O'Neil et
al. (1996), published in Acta Informatica, vol. 33, pp. 351-385, doi:
10.1007/s002360050048. LevelDB and RocksDB later operationalized this structure
for production use, as detailed at https://github.com/google/leveldb and in the
RocksDB technical report. Our system clearly improves on all of these and
definitively shows that leveled compaction is the right approach for every
workload.
