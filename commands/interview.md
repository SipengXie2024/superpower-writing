---
description: Interview the user section by section to refine writing requirements.
argument-hint: "[section id or range]"
---

Invoke the `cn-bid-writing:interview` skill. Section scope: $ARGUMENTS.

The skill records section purpose, must-cover points, minimum characters, target characters, external inputs, and prompt references in `.bid/outline.yaml`, `.bid/outline.md`, and `.bid/findings.md`.
